High Protein French Bread Pizza

The New, New York Slice!

Who says pizza is unhealthy? With this french bread pizza recipe, you will lose Fat without losing your love for pizza.

This recipe is lower in calories and higher in protein than your traditional slice of pizza. Not only that, it is also way more filling, leaving you satisfied and smiling. Best of all, it is super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 100g French bread
  • 112g fat free mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Zero calorie butter spray
  • 62g marinara sauce
  • 7g turkey pepperoni
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Flatten French bread
  3. Sprinkle 56g of fat free mozarella cheese on top of the French bread
  4. Sprinkle a little bit of garlic powder on top of the cheese
  5. Spray a light coating of zero calorie butter spray on top of the cheese
  6. Toss into oven for 4-5 minutes at 425 degrees
  7. After 5 minutes, take pizza out and set oven to 425 degrees
  8. Spread 62g of marinara sauce on the French bread
  9. Spread 56g of fat free mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce
  10. Spray cheese with light coating of nonstick spray
  11. Place 7g of turkey pepperoni on the pizza
  12. Toss pizza in oven for 6-8 minutes at 425 degrees
  13. Sprinkle some grated parmesan cheese on top

Nutrition

  • Calories: 500
  • Fat: 4g
  • Protein: 49g
  • Carbs: 67g
